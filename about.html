About me

Hi!

Hi! I am Léo Bernard, also known as Yolwoocle. I'm from France and I love creating colorful and lively things, and I hope to bring people joy. I've loved making stuff since I was a small boy, spending hours creating games on Scratch and stop motion animations on iPad.

I am currently studying mathematics and computer science in France.

I have experimented with tools like Unity, Godot, and LÖVE. I enjoy cozy coding environments, like PICO-8.

Contact me

Here you can find various ways to contact me. You can be certain that I will read your message, however don't worry if I don't answer because I am quite busy.

You can find the source code for my projects on Github. If you want to reuse part of my stuff in your personal projects, video or review, do not hesitate to do so. If your project is commercial or public, please email me first. I will probably say yes! Please do NOT reupload my games elsewhere on the Internet without my permission first.
