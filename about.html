About me

Hi!

Hi! I am Léo Bernard, also known as Yolwoocle. I'm a developer from France and I enjoy making games just as much as birds enjoy throwing objects off desks. I love the joy of creating and I want to make colorful and lively things that bring people joy. I've loved making stuff since I was a small boy, spending hours creating games on Scratch and stop motion animations on my parents iPad.

I am currently studying mathematics and computer science in French preparatory classes (MP2I) at the Lycée du Parc in Lyon, France.

I have experimented with tools like Python, Unity, and LÖVE. I enjoy cozy coding environments, like PICO-8.
