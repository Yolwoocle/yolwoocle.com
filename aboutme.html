Hi!

I am Léo Bernard, also known as Yolwoocle. I love making games just as much as cats love pushing objects off desks. I am passionate about maths and making games. Over the years, I have experimented with tools like Python, Unity, and LÖVE. I enjoy cozy coding environments, like PICO-8.
